Welcome

The Life Skills Program helps to develope black men seeking positive direction.

I believe lifes experience provides an opportunity to give back and be a part of a solution throughout the challanges that black men may face in there lifetime.

It takes dedication and will to achieve goals. Listening, guiding and supporting is critical. Through discussion I explore many areas that help build a solid foundation. Here are a few.

Self Awareness

Understanding ones self in character, feelings, motives.

Accountability

The obligation or willingness to accept responsibility or to account for one's actions.

Reliabilaty

The ability to be relied on or depended on, as for accuracy, honesty, or achievement.

Health and Wellness

Taking care of the body and mind.

Actions and Consequence

What to do? Why do it? What is the result?

Staying Focused

Setting positive goals and remaining firm in their undertaking in spite of counterinfluences, opposition, or discouragement.
